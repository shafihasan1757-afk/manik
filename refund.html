Refund Policy

Please read our refund policy before placing your order.

1. Refund Eligibility

A refund may be considered only if your order has not been started or if we are unable to deliver the purchased service.

2. Non-Refundable Orders

Completed orders and successfully delivered services are generally not eligible for a refund.

3. Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed within a reasonable time after verification.

4. Replacement Policy

If an eligible service drops within the stated warranty period, we will review it for a refill or replacement where applicable.

5. Customer Responsibilities

Customers must provide the correct profile or post link and follow our service instructions. Incorrect information may affect delivery and refund eligibility.

6. Refund Not Available

Refunds are generally not available if:

✔ The order has been completed successfully.
✔ Wrong information was provided by the customer.
✔ The customer changes their mind after the order starts.
✔ The issue is caused by changes made by Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, TikTok or other platforms.

7. Dispute Resolution

If you have any issue with your order, please contact our support team before opening any payment dispute. We will do our best to resolve the problem quickly.

8. Policy Updates

Social Orbit BD reserves the right to update this Refund Policy at any time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
